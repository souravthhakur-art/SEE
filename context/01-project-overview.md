## What Palum Dhara Is

Palum Dhara is a premium Indian food and lifestyle brand. We create and sell curated food products and lifestyle goods that reflect Indian artistry, craftsmanship, and everyday elegance. The name draws from the idea of flow — of tradition into modern life.

## Mission

To make refined, artful living accessible to Indian consumers beyond the metros — without diluting quality, authenticity, or aesthetic integrity.

## Vision

To become the defining Indian food and lifestyle brand for the next decade: rooted in tradition, designed for today, built for scale.

## Business Model

| Channel | Model |
|---|---|
| DTC Website | Primary revenue — direct-to-consumer ecommerce |
| Subscriptions (My Pantry) | Recurring revenue through curated product deliveries and active pantry subscriptions |
| Future | Marketplace presence, wholesale (post-launch) |

## Target Audience

Indian premium consumers. Young families. Professionals. Gift buyers. Health-conscious households. People who value authenticity more than status.

The website should always feel welcoming, never intimidating.

## Product Categories

- Food products (primary)
- Home and living
- Personal care
- Gifting
- Curated collections (seasonal/festive)

Specific SKUs and pricing are not documented here. Refer to product data sources.

## Website Goals

- **Convert** — Clear path from discovery to purchase
- **Build trust** — Brand storytelling, product photography, transparent information
- **Retain** — Subscription model, newsletter, account experience
- **Perform** — Fast loading, smooth interactions, mobile-first

## Homepage Strategy

Current v1 section hierarchy (subject to iteration based on analytics):

1. Trust
2. Shop
3. Subscriptions
4. Story
5. Journal

Commerce leads. Content supports. This sequence can change as we learn from real user behavior, but the principle remains: every section should ultimately help users understand, trust, and purchase.

## Launch Goals

- Ship a functional, polished v1 of the ecommerce experience
- Establish brand presence and visual identity online
- Validate product-market fit with initial audience
- Collect data and feedback for iteration
